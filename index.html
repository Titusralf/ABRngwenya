About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1982, ABR Ngwenya Business Enterprise has been a family-owned business dedicated to helping families create meaningful and lasting memorials for their loved ones. Our craftsmen combine traditional techniques with modern technology to create pieces that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

We believe that every life deserves to be remembered with dignity and beauty. Our mission is to provide families with memorials that not only honor their loved ones but also bring comfort and peace during difficult times.

Why Choose Us?

With over four decades of experience, we understand the importance of quality, attention to detail, and compassionate service. We work closely with each family to create a memorial that truly reflects the life and personality of their loved one.
